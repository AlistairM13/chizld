## Decisions Made

- GestureHandlerRootView placed outside SQLiteProvider (must be the absolute outermost native wrapper for gestures to work throughout the tree)
- useElapsedTimer uses absolute `Date.now()` diff rather than accumulation to prevent drift in long sessions
- RPESlider uses `Gesture.Race(pan, tap)` so both dragging and tap-to-jump work, with pan taking priority during drag
- Thumb x-position calculated by `rpeToX()` which maps RPE value to pixel coordinate — keeps gesture math and display math separate
