# Phase 02.1 Plan 01: Zone Label and Photo Slot Components Summary

**One-liner:** Floating ZoneLabel text and RPG-style PhotoSlot with warm/cold color states

## What Was Built

### ZoneLabel Component
- Floating text display (no background or border)
- Zone name: Barlow Condensed SemiBold, 14px, uppercase
- Level: JetBrains Mono Regular, 11px, format "LV.{n}"
- Warm state: ember[500] name, ember[300] level
- Cold state: text.secondary name, text.muted level
- Side-aware alignment: left zones align right, right zones align left

### PhotoSlot Component
- 50x50 default square (configurable via size prop)
- Cold state: 1px dashed grey border, grey "+" icon, no glow
- Warm state: 1px solid ember border, ember "+" icon, subtle glow (shadowRadius: 6, elevation: 4)
- Photo state: Image fills slot with cover resizeMode
- Pressable wrapper for future tap handling
- Shadow container for Android elevation support
